About Our School

Welcome to Leaders Academy, where we are committed to raising the next generation of thinkers, innovators, and leaders. Located in the heart of Iba New Town, Lagos, our school offers a vibrant and inclusive learning environment designed to nurture excellence in every child.

Since our founding, we have focused on academic achievement, character building, and personal growth. Our dedicated teachers, modern facilities, and commitment to innovation ensure that every student receives a balanced and world-class education.

At Leaders Academy, we prepare students not just to pass exams, but to thrive in life. Our programs include solid preparation for WAEC and JAMB, hands-on computer and tech skills, and enrichment activities to build confidence and discipline.

We believe every child has the potential to succeed, and we are here to guide them every step of the way. Join us and be a part of a school that truly makes a difference.
