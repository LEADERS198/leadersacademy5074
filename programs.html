Our Programs

Empowering Students with Knowledge and Confidence

WAEC and NECO Preparatory Classes

Our WAEC and NECO classes are designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge they need to pass their exams excellently. We use updated syllabuses, experienced tutors, and regular mock tests to ensure mastery of all subjects.

JAMB/UTME Intensive Coaching

With a focus on speed, accuracy, and comprehension, our JAMB classes prepare students to score high in UTME. We provide CBT practice, past questions, and exam simulations to boost their confidence and performance.

Junior Secondary School Support

We help Junior Secondary students build a strong foundation in English, Mathematics, Basic Science, and Social Studies. Our small-group learning method helps weaker students catch up quickly and develop self-belief.

Computer Training Programs

In a digital age, computer literacy is essential. Our computer training includes Microsoft Office, Graphics Design, Web Development, and Typing skills. Every student gets hands-on experience in a well-equipped computer lab.

Kindergaten/Nursery/Basic Classes

We are determined to catch them young and inculcate academic, good morals, inventive and innovative mindset into the younger generation. At leaders academy, your child/ward learns from an early age, the technologies of the future.

Holiday Booster Classes

During school breaks, we offer holiday booster sessions to help students revise and get ahead. These fun and engaging classes keep learners sharp and motivated throughout the year.

Adult Education & Remedial Classes

For adults who want to return to school or improve their academic skills, we offer literacy programs, evening lessons, and exam preparation classes tailored to their schedules and goals.
